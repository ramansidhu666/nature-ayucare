About us

Registered Massage Therapists at the Natural Ayu Care are health care professionals committed to restoring and maintain optimal health and pain-free function of your body. Our certified therapists accurately assess and treat with techniques that include massage and manual therapy, joint mobilization, hydrotherapy, and rehabilitative exercise such as stretching, strengthening, postural exercise, and patient education.

We are proud to provide outstanding care in many modalities of massage therapy and wellness services. We put our clients first. Our staff works together to ensure you get the best possible massage experience. Whether you visit for relaxing massage, stress or pain relief, our experienced practitioner will ensure you get the most out of you massage. Our Philosophy: At Natural Ayu Care, our team of certified professional massage therapists are dedicated to delivering the ultimate relaxation experience you deserve. Friendly Care: You will receive an unhurried and thorough treatment, always with a caring attitude. Patient trust and confidentiality is always respected.

Bio

Avtar graduated from York University with honors Bachelor of Kinesiology, massage therapy diploma at National Institute. Continuing education is an important component to staying active in our profession. He is a member of Ontario Herbalist Association as Registered Herbalist. He is committed to regular upgrading in order to give you the benefit of the most current research and treatments. In addition, he has worked in physiotherapy clinics to facilitate pain reduction, healing, bring a renewed sense of balance into your life, particularly in brining comfort to older bodies. He specializes in postural imbalances as well as sports and work-related injuries. With the ability to offer advice in English, Punjabi, or Hindi, Avtar is excited to talk your language and make you feel comfortable during your massage. Avtar is looking forward to the future and continues to be up to date on his training and new massage techniques; he brings exceptional service for all and wishes to help build a strong and healthy community.
